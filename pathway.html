Your Pathway to Lifesaving Certifications

Certification Pathway

Bronze Star

First step for lifesaving skills. No prerequisites required.

Bronze Medallion

Develop lifesaving knowledge. Prerequisite: Bronze Star or age 13.

Bronze Cross

Enhance skills and prepare for National Lifeguard. Prerequisite: Bronze Medallion.

National Lifeguard

Professional lifeguard certification. Prerequisites: Bronze Cross, Standard First Aid, and age 15.

Swim Instructor

Teach swimming skills to all levels. Prerequisites: Bronze Cross or National Lifeguard.

Lifesaving Instructor

Train others in lifesaving skills. Prerequisite: Swim Instructor certification.
