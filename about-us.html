About Us

Our Story

Founded by Eren U, Lifeguardix started with a mission to empower individuals with life-saving skills. With years of experience in lifesaving and first aid, Eren envisioned a platform where comprehensive training and quality education could be accessible to everyone.

Over the years, Lifeguardix has become a trusted name in lifesaving certifications, partnering with schools, workplaces, and communities across the country. Our team of certified instructors is dedicated to providing hands-on training and theoretical knowledge to prepare you for real-life emergencies.

Our Vision

We believe in a world where everyone has the skills to save a life. Whether it's a lifeguard at a pool, a teacher in a classroom, or a parent at home, our goal is to equip as many people as possible with the knowledge and confidence to act when it matters most.

Why Choose Us?

Lifeguardix is accredited by the Lifesaving Society, ensuring that all our programs meet the highest industry standards. We take pride in offering interactive, practical, and enjoyable learning experiences tailored to your needs.
