Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses and certifications

What are the prerequisites for your courses?

Each course has specific prerequisites. For example, Bronze Medallion requires candidates to be at least 13 years old or have completed the Bronze Star certification. Check the individual course pages for details.

Are your courses accredited?

Yes, all our courses are accredited by the Lifesaving Society, ensuring they meet national and international standards.

How do I register for a course?

Visit our course pages to find the program you're interested in. Currently, registration is not available, but links and instructions will be updated once registration opens.

What should I bring to the course?

Bring government-issued photo identification, swimwear, a towel, and a notebook. Some courses may require additional materials, which will be detailed in the course information.

Do you offer recertification courses?

Yes, we offer recertification courses for National Lifeguard, Standard First Aid, and CPR certifications. Check our course pages for availability and prerequisites.

How long are the certifications valid?

Certifications are typically valid for two to three years, depending on the course. Standard First Aid and CPR certifications require recertification every three years, while National Lifeguard certifications require renewal every two years.

Where are the courses held?

Our courses are conducted at certified training centers and pools. Locations will be listed during the registration process.
